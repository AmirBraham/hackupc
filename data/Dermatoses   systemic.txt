 Dermatoses is a general term used to describe any skin defect or lesion on the skin. Systemic means it affects the whole body, rather than just one part. Systemic dermatoses may be linked to problems in other organs.Examples of the most common dermatoses include:DermatomyositisErythema nodosumPemphigus vulgarisPsoriasisSclerodermaSweet syndromeSystemic lupus erythematosusVitiligoImages
