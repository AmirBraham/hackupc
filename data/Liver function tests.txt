 Liver function tests are common tests that are used to see how well the liver is working. Tests include:AlbuminAlpha-1 antitrypsinAlkaline phosphatase (ALP)Alanine aminotransferase (ALT)Aspartate aminotransferase (AST)Gamma-glutamyl transpeptidase (GGT)5' nucleotidaseProthrombin time, also expressed as international normalized ratio (INR)Serum bilirubinUrine bilirubinAlternative Names
