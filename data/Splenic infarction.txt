 Splenic infarction is the death of tissue (necrosis) in the spleen due to a blockage in blood flow.Causes

Common causes of splenic infarction include:Blood clots from the heartBlood cancer, such as lymphoma or leukemiaDiseases in which the blood clots more easily, such as sickle cell diseaseInflammation of the pancreas or a mass in the pancreas pressing on the blood vessels to the spleenImages
