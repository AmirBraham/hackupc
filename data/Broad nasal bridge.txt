 Broad nasal bridge is a widening of the top part of the nose.Considerations

Broad nasal bridge can be a normal facial feature. However, it can also be associated with certain genetic or congenital (present from birth) disorders.Causes

Causes may include:Basal cell nevus syndromeFetal hydantoin effect (mother took the drug hydantoin during pregnancy)Normal facial featureOther congenital syndromesHome Care

There is no need to treat broad nasal bridge. Other conditions that have broad nasal bridge as a symptom may need medical attention.When to Contact a Medical Professional

Contact your health care provider if:You feel that the shape of your child's nose is interfering with breathingYou have questions about your child's noseWhat to Expect at Your Office Visit

The provider will perform a physical exam. Provider may also ask questions about the person's family and medical history.Images
