 Metastasis is the movement or spreading of cancer cells from one organ or tissue to another. Cancer cells usually spread through the blood or the lymph system.If a cancer spreads, it is said to have "metastasized."Information

Whether or not cancer cells spread to other parts of the body depends on many things, including:The type of cancerThe stage of the cancerOriginal location of the cancer Your body's natural defenses, such as the immune systemTreatment depends on the type of cancer and where it has spread.Alternative Names
