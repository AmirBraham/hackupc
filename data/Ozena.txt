 Ozena is a disease of the nose in which the bony ridges and mucous membranes of the nose waste away.Ozena is also called atrophic rhinitis.Symptoms

Nasal crusting, discharge, congestion, and a very bad smell are other common symptoms of ozena.Ozena may develop after a long period of nasal swelling.Images
