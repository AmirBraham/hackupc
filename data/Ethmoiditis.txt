 Ethmoiditis is an infection or inflammation of the ethmoidal cells in the sinuses. These are the air-filled spaces behind the nose and between the eyes.Ethmoiditis is a type of sinusitis.Causes

Bacteria cause most ethmoid sinus infections. Some types of fungus and viruses can also be involved.Symptoms

Symptoms usually include:Headache between the eyes Runny noseNasal congestionLoss of sense of smellSymptoms of more serious infections can include: Vision lossDouble visionRedness or bulging of the eyes Images
