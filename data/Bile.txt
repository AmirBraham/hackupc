 Bile is a fluid that is made and released by the liver and stored in the gallbladder.Bile helps with digestion. It breaks down fats into fatty acids, which can be taken into the body by the digestive tract.Bile contains:Mostly cholesterolBile acids (also called bile salts)Bilirubin (a breakdown product or red blood cells)It also contains:WaterBody salts (such as potassium and sodium)Copper and other metals (attached to proteins)Images
